Arrabbiata

Description

Don't be fooled by the arrabbiata's appearance, it packs a huge kick of heat under its tomato guise.

Intensified by actual chili and chili flakes, this angry dish is sure to keep your tongue crying for mercy.

    Ingredients
  • Penne
  • Parmigiano reggiano
  • Chili
  • Chili flakes
  • Pureed tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Olive oil
  • Water
    Steps
  1. Get a pot of water boiling.
  2. Slice up garlic and chili thinnly. Grate parmigiano reggiano finely and set aside.
  3. Heat up a pan to medium heat and pour in some olive oil.
  4. Add in sliced garlic, chili and chili flakes. Stir fry until garlic begins to brown.
  5. Add pureed tomatoes and basil. Lower the heat once the sauce starts to bubble.
  6. While the sauce reduces, add penne to the pot and cook until al dente.
  7. Remove the basil from the pan just before penne finishes cooking.
  8. Strain the penne and add it into the pan with the sauce. Add pasta water to the pan to adjust consistency if needed.
  9. Marry penne with the sauce until the sauce reduces slightly.
  10. Turn off the heat, add in parmigiano reggiano and mix well.
  11. Plate the pasta and garnish with more parmigiano reggiano. Serve while warm.
